Binarygeek119 Ready-made Channels

Creates the standard Binarygeek119 TV lineup with channel numbers, names, library tags, and bundled logos from open-channel-logos (fintv2) for both the Live TV guide icon and on-screen channel bug.

Legacy mode uses the original Binarygeek119 dial positions. Subchannel mode groups channels under main numbers with .1–.9 slots.

Channel bugs live under Shows, Movies, and Music Videos Channels. The EBS/ folder is separate: those full-screen slates play when a channel has no scheduled media, with background music from your Jellyfin music libraries (configured in Live TV Setup).

Jellyfin Live TV URLs

Jellyfin fetches these URLs from the server itself. They must not require login and must use an address Jellyfin can reach (not localhost unless Jellyfin runs on the same machine and resolves it).

When a viewer tunes in and the channel has no media scheduled, FinTV shows an EBS slate and plays random audio from the selected source. Create a library named Background Music or choose any music library.
